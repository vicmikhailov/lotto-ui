# Coverlot

Winning Lotto Max means picking 7 numbers from 50, which creates 99,884,400 possible jackpot combinations. Lotto 6/49 is no less daunting, with 6 numbers from 49 for 13,983,816 possible combinations. To put that in perspective, the odds of being struck by lightning are often estimated at about 1 in 135,000 over a lifetime, while the odds of dying in a car crash are still far higher than most people realize. In other words, a lottery jackpot is the kind of prize most people will never see.
